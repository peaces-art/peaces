Who I am

About Me

I'm an illustrator, painter, and graphic designer based in the Pacific Northwest, with freelance experience across a wide range of creative projects.

I've been drawing for as long as I can remember — born in December 1994, my first ever drawing was a trapezoid, and I haven't stopped since.

My favourite things to design are t-shirts, logos, and custom paintings — especially portraits of my favourite artists. Every piece I make starts from the heart and is built with intention.
